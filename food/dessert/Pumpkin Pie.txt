Pumpkin Pie 
(based on Betty Crocker's)
Preheat oven to 425ºF

9" pie

1 pie crust pastry
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
15oz can pumpkin
12oz can evaporated milk
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2" finely chopped or shredded ginger
8 crushed cloves
1/2 teaspoon allspice
3/4 teaspoon fresh ground nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon salt

Prepare pie crust dough. Beat eggs by hand, mix in remaining ingredients. Pour into crust and bake for 15 minutes.

Reduce oven to 350ºF, bake about 45 minutes until knife comes out clean.

Refrigerate 4 hours.

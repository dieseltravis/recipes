Grandma Pat's Rum Cake
Preheat oven to 325º

½ cup chopped walnuts
1 18½oz package yellow cake mix
1 3.4oz package instant vanilla pudding
3 eggs
½ cup cold water
½ cup wesson oil
½ cup dark rum
butter & flour to coat pan

Grease & flour 12 cup bundt pan. Sprinkle nuts over bottom of pan. Mix
all cake ingredients together. Pour batter over nuts in pan. Bake 1
hour. Set on rack to cool. Invert on plate & prick top & sides with
toothpick. Drizzle & brush glaze over top & sides.

Glaze

⅛ lb. Butter
⅛ cup water
½ cup granulated sugar
¼ cup rum

Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in water & sugar. Boil 5 minutes
stirring constantly. Stir in rum.
